About Lernia

Helping students choose with clarity.

Lernia was created to bring universities, cost of living and scholarship information into one place, so that big decisions do not depend on endless spreadsheets or dozens of open browser tabs.

Our mission

Bring transparency to studying abroad.

Studying abroad changes careers, finances and everyday life. Our focus is to cut through the noise and give you an honest view of destinations, programmes and financial support opportunities.

We want every student to be able to compare paths fairly: not only rankings and tuition fees, but also cost of living, scholarships and local context.
